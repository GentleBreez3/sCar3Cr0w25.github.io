Pig-Dice-Game

Pig-Dice-Game is a simple dice game where on a turn, a player rolls the die repeatedly. The goal is to accumulate as many points as possible, adding up the numbers rolled on the die. However, if a player rolls a 1, the player's turn is over and any points they have accumulated during this turn are forfeited.When a player reaches a total of 100 or more points, the game ends and that player is the winner.
